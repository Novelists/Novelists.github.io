Requirements to Join!

We are recruiting both F2P and P2P with any total level and any combat. No experience needed to join us!

Ranking Up!

To gain rank in the clan, you should be active and involved with the activities and events held by clan members. Each event attended earns points for the participants.

Events can be held by any member of the general clan, as long as there are a minimum of 3 Novelist members in attendance.

Events held by members will be counted towards Rank-Ups, assuming that proper attendance is posted on the forums by a ranked member- confirming that the event has taken place.

What Events Do We Do?

Everything! Anything your heart desires! Just throw it into chat, get a couple people intersted, and report the attendace! Want to cut yew for a few hours? Rocktail? Dunge? No Problem!

Member Responsibilities

We expect you to be an active, positive force in the development of the clan atmosphere and community. While you are not required to cap resources at the citadel, It may be hosted as an event, and also earns a bonus point towards rank up!
